  • From Jiangxi to California: Seeking peace on the go

    Published April 15, 2017

    I was born and raised in Jiangxi Province, China. Inspired by the creativity of the nature, I dedicated my life to seeking the harmony between design and humanity. I like colors because they remind me of the view when I was on top of Mountain Jinggangshan with the flow of the strips of the wind and the floating clouds that refracts lights into speechless colors. I like UI design because it's the result of understanding, consideration and efficiency, which I learn the most from the nature. California gave me the similar harmonious feeling: One hour driving to the Atlantic beaches, one hour driving to the Rocky mountains; live cities like San Francisco and San Jose, National Parks like Yosemite. All these feature the harmony of Cali life.
